Who Done It?

How to Play:

Welcome to my text adventure game! This style of this game is based off a murder mystery. To play this game you simply type in commands in the text box. The web page will alert you if your decision works or not. The map on the webpage is the layout of the house and there are different items to interact with. Hope you enjoy!

Situation: Tonight is your friend Jeff's birthday. Jeff has invited a couple of his friends to celebrate at his small three room house. This includes Jasmine, Fred, Carl, Zoe, and you. While you are all celebrating in the living room and the power goes out. The power turns back on immedietly and you realize Jeff is gone!!! Everyone begins to panic. In the midst of the panic you find a note on the ground.


The note says "I have killed Jeff and hid his body. If you find him, I will reveal myself and turnmyself in. Good luck."


Your friends all faint to the news and cant be woken up. You are now responsible for finding Jeff's body and murderer.

Objective: FIND JEFF'S BODY AND CATCH THE KILLER.



Tips for playing the game:

  1. Use commands like "walk in blank", "pick up blank", "look at blank", "go to blank"
  2. The command "show inventory" will alert you what is in your inventory.
  3. Have fun!
