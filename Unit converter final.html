Welcome to my conversion project! Below are some conversion boxes to fill in. Hope you enjoy!

*Converter only works when you put values in the left hand side.

Fahrenheit to Celcius:

Enter your fahrenheit value and it will convert to celcius.

Feet to Inches:

Enter the amount of feet and it will convert to inches.

Inches to Centimeters:

Enter your inch(es) value and it will convert to centimeters.

Feet to Yards:

Enter the amount of feet and it will convert to yards.
